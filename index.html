About Me

My name is Wayne Corbita, and I am from Isabela City, Basilan. I grew up on a vibrant island
filled with rich culture, warm communities, and breathtaking natural beauty. My experiences
growing up in this unique environment have shaped who I am today, instilling in me a deep
appreciation for diversity and resilience. I carry the spirit of my island with me whereverI go.
In my free time, I love listening to music, watching movies, and spending quality time with my
family and friends. These moments bring me joy and help me stay connected to the people I care about most.

Academically, I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, where I am constantly
honing my skills in various programming languages and development methodologies. My coursework
and personal projects have provided me with a strong foundation in web development, including
front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as an understanding of back-end
principles. I am always eager to learn new technologies and expand my knowledge base to tackle more
complex challenges.

I am passionate about creating impactful and user-friendly digital experiences. My goal is to leverage
my technical skills and creative problem-solving abilities to contribute to innovative projects
and make a meaningful difference. I am a dedicated and collaborative individual, always striving for
excellence and committed to delivering high-quality work.
